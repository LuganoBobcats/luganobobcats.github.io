Who We Are

Born from Nothing.
Built on Love.

The Lugano Bobcats didn't start with money, a facility, or a sponsor. We started with a ball, a broken court, and the belief that basketball teaches what schools often can't — resilience, brotherhood, and the discipline to get back up.

We exist because the streets of Lugano deserve better. Because kids need a place to go, a game to play, and someone to show them that the world doesn't have to be as ridiculous as it seems.

This campaign is about education through sport and money. We want people to understand that small, honest contributions — directed with clarity and accountability — can rebuild communities from the ground up.

"My friend built amoreperilcancro before he left us. He poured everything into that charity. This campaign is my way of finally honoring what he started — making sure his work reaches people who need it, through the sport that raised me."

— Founder, Lugano Bobcats

On June 13th, 2026, we play the 3x3 Ticinotour Finals in Lugano. That's our deadline. That's our stage. Whatever we raise by then goes out — no delays, no excuses.

Questions

We Have Nothing to Hide

Who are the Lugano Bobcats? +
An amateur basketball club from Lugano, Ticino. We started from nothing — no facility, no sponsor, no institutional backing. Just people who believe sport can rebuild streets and lives.
Who is amoreperilcancro? +
A cancer charity founded by a close friend of our founder before he passed away from the illness. Half of every donation goes directly to this charity as a tribute to him and the work he started.
How do I know my money goes where you say? +
The founder maintains a public ledger of all receipts and transfers. Everything is documented. If you ever want to verify — email us and you'll get a response within 24 hours, with proof.
What happens on June 13th? +
The 3x3 Ticinotour Finals in Lugano. We play, we distribute balls and orange t-shirts to kids in the community, and we make the donation to amoreperilcancro publicly, in person.
What if you don't reach 1 million francs? +
Then we reach whatever we reach — and every franc of it still goes exactly where we said. The million was a dream. The mission is real regardless of the number.
Can Adidas actually just send equipment? +
Yes. Please. We're serious. Email us at the contact below. We'll make sure it gets to the right kids.
