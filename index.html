01 — Who We Are

Born from Nothing.
Building Everything.

The Lugano Bobcats started without a facility, without a sponsor, without anyone asking us to exist. We showed up on a court that needed work and decided to make something out of it anyway.

This campaign is about what happens when a community moves together. A new gym. New playgrounds in the streets of Lugano. Basketballs for kids who have never had a real one. Sneakers for the ones who need them. Orange t-shirts so every kid knows they belong to something.

We are lucky to play sport. Not everyone is. This campaign exists to change that — to take what we have been given and multiply it for the next generation coming up in Ticino.

On June 13th, 2026, we play the 3x3 Ticinotour Finals in Lugano. That is our deadline. Whatever we raise goes out that day — no delays, no excuses, full public ledger.

"We are not asking for charity. We are asking people to invest in what their city can become when sport is truly accessible to everyone."

— Founder, Lugano Bobcats

Also donating to amoreperilcancro — a Ticino cancer charity that deserves more reach and more support. 50% of everything raised goes directly there, transferred on June 13th with full public documentation.

02 — Transparency

What Is a Ledger?

A ledger is simply a written record of every franc that comes in and every franc that goes out. Think of it like a notebook where you write down: who gave money, when, and how much — and then separately, what it was spent on, when, and with proof attached.

Banks use ledgers. Governments use ledgers. Companies use ledgers. Now so do we — because when you donate to something, you deserve to know exactly where your money landed.

Our ledger has two columns: In (donations received) and Out (money spent or transferred). Every entry has a date, a description, and a receipt or transfer confirmation.

It is public. Ask to see it any time — we reply within 24 hours. No exceptions, no surprises.

Who are the Lugano Bobcats?+
An amateur basketball club from Lugano, Switzerland. No institutional backing, no professional sponsors. Built by people who believe sport should be accessible to every kid in this city, regardless of what they can afford.
What is amoreperilcancro?+
A cancer charity based in Ticino. Half of every donation from this campaign goes directly to them — transferred on June 13th with full public documentation available to anyone who asks.
What is a ledger and how can I see it?+
A ledger is a full record of every franc in and every franc out — dates, amounts, recipients, and receipts attached. Ours is public. Email us any time and you will receive the complete document within 24 hours. No exceptions.
How do I donate with TWINT?+
Open your Swiss banking app (UBS, PostFinance, Raiffeisen, Cantonal bank — they all have TWINT built in). Go to TWINT, scan our QR code or send to our TWINT number. The donation arrives instantly. We log it in the ledger and confirm by email.
How do I donate with crypto?+
Click the Bitcoin or Ethereum address in the Donate section to copy it. Open your crypto wallet app (Coinbase, Trust Wallet, MetaMask, or any other), choose "Send", paste our address, enter any amount, and confirm. Then email us the transaction ID so we can verify it and add it to the ledger.
What if you don't reach CHF 1,000,000?+
Then we reach whatever we reach — and every franc of it goes exactly where we said. The million is the dream we said out loud. The mission works at any amount. One donation buys two balls. That is real and it counts.
Can Adidas just send equipment?+
Yes. Please. We are serious. We have kids, we have a date, and we have a court. Email us and we will make it happen.
